Learning

W3Schools is one of my favorite places to learn how to code. It makes finding the syntax for lots of different languages easy. I also remember using Code.org when I was younger to learn some of the basics of coding. Stack Overflow has also been useful when working on more complex or personal projects.

I've also found it useful to look at the documentation given by the creators of languages and libraries. I think the ones I've visited the most are the Bootstrap documentation(by a mile) and the documentation for MongoDB and C#.
